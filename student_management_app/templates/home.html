FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Can't find what you need? Reach out to us.

Is StudentMS free to use? +
Yes! StudentMS offers a generous free tier suitable for small institutions. For larger schools with advanced features like bulk CSV import, analytics, and priority support, we offer affordable paid plans.
How do I add students and teachers in bulk? +
Simply prepare a CSV file with the required columns (name, email, class, etc.) and upload it via the Admin Dashboard. StudentMS will automatically create accounts and send login credentials to each user.
Can parents track their child's attendance and results? +
Absolutely. Parents can be given read-only access to their child's profile where they can see attendance records, exam results, and receive automatic notifications for important updates.
Is my institution's data secure? +
Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256. We comply with GDPR standards and perform regular security audits. Your data is never shared with third parties.
Does it work on mobile devices? +
Yes! StudentMS is fully responsive and works smoothly on all screen sizes — desktop, tablet, and mobile. A dedicated mobile app is also on our roadmap.
What kind of support do you offer? +
We provide email support for all users, live chat support for paid plans, and a dedicated account manager for enterprise institutions. Our average response time is under 2 hours.

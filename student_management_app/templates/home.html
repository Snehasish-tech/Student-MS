Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some of the common questions about our platform and onboarding process.

How easy is it to set up?

Extremely. Our platform comes with pre-defined structures and role-based onboarding that simplifies setup from hours to just minutes.

Can we customize our modules?

Definitely. You can tailor modules for attendance tracking, result management, and administrative workflows according to your institution's custom needs.

Is student data secure?

Yes. Data security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption protocols and secure database systems to protect all student and staff information.

Who can access the platform?

We provide exclusive access to three primary roles: HOD (Admin), Staff (Teachers), and Students, each with their own specialized dashboards and permission levels.
